Mend — The Digital Mirror

A gentle space for self-reflection and emotional healing

Mend is a web platform where users can:

  • Write freely about their feelings.
  • Receive AI-guided reflection — a short empathetic summary, gentle advice, and emotion tags.
  • Explore relevant quotes, poems, music, films, and scripture matched to their emotion.
  • Save reflections to track growth over time.
The platform becomes a private sanctuary for emotional honesty — a dialogue between the human heart and a compassionate digital mirror.
